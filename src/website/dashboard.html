Economy

/balance [user]
Show a premium image wallet card with cash, bank, level, XP, and rank.
/profile [user]
Full profile card plus badge showcase and recent transaction history.
/daily
Claim your daily treat. Streak multiplier up to ×2.4 at day 7.
/work
Work a shift at your current café job and earn coins + XP.
/job list · info · apply · quit
Browse and manage your career — from barista all the way to café owner.
/shop [category]
Browse consumables, upgrades, and collectibles in the café boutique.
/buy · /sell · /use · /inventory
Purchase items, sell them back, activate consumables, or inspect your bag.
/bank deposit · withdraw · upgrade
Manage your tiered vault (Tin Jar → Diamond Vault) with daily interest.
/lottery info · buy
Buy tickets for the weekly café lottery draw. Pot rolls over each week.
/crime · /rob [user]
Try your luck at petty crime or robbing another user's wallet.
/pay [user] [amount]
Transfer coins directly to another server member.
/leaderboard · /networth · /transactions
See the rich list image card, your net worth rank, or your TX history.

Leveling

/level [user]
Check current XP, level, and rank on this server.
/level-leaderboard
Paginated leaderboard for the current server's XP rankings.
/levelpanel
Interactive admin panel to configure XP, cooldowns, roles, and announcements.
/levelconfig toggle · multiplier · cooldown
Fine-tune XP gain per-server without opening the full panel.

Moderation

/kick · /ban · /unban
Standard member management actions, logged to mod-log channel.
/warn · /warnings · /clearwarnings
Issue, view, and clear warnings for any server member.
/mute · /tempmute · /unmute
Silence members temporarily or indefinitely.
/purge [amount]
Bulk-delete up to 100 messages at once.
/automod
Interactive dashboard: anti-spam, anti-link, bad words, mass mention, raid protection.
/whitelist add · remove
Exempt users or roles from all automod checks.

Music

/play [query or URL]
Queue a track from YouTube, Spotify, or direct URL via Lavalink.
/queue · /skip · /stop · /pause · /resume
Full playback control with a live now-playing card.
/loop · /shuffle · /volume
Loop modes (off / track / queue), shuffle queue, set volume.
/nowplaying
Live progress-bar card for the current track with interactive buttons.
